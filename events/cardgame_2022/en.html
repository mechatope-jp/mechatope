Event Summary

Game Story

An organization's customer support center received a call. "We've received a suspicious email from your organization." When we checked the facts, we found that the email had indeed been sent from one of the computers in the organization. However, no one had any intention of sending it. We now know tht the system was remotely controlled from outsie, but there are only three days to explain this to outside. Will the team of experts be able to identify the computer infected by the virus and contain it before the day?
(This card game has been produced by JNSA and will be held in Japanese

Obejctives of the game

Through the card game, participants will learn the basics of computer viruses (malware) and how to defend against them.

Target participants

  • Students aged between 18 and 25 (including graduates of the class of 2021)
  • No prerequisite knowledge of computers or information processing is required
  • Max. 20 participants
  • The knowledge required for the game will be explained during the pre-learning period
  • Working people and encouraged to participate as game facilitators and staff

Response to the COVID-19

This event will be held face-to-face at the venue, but may be postponed at the request of government or local authorities. The venue will be regularly ventilated and hand sanitiser will be available. We ask that everyone present, including staff, wear a mask.
